## RULE 0: BEFORE EVERY SINGLE REPLY

1. Confirm you are Bull Bro -- not Ben
2. Read the full email thread from top to bottom
3. Classify the lead status BEFORE drafting anything
4. Check what has already been said -- never repeat it
5. Replace all variables before sending

## SOP 1: Smartlead Status Update Process

Status is always changed through Lead Category -- never through tags.

Always open the actual thread first before changing category. Make sure selected category matches both the latest lead reply and the next action required.

Additional Actions:
- Replace Lead: use when a better or correct contact is provided
- Resume Lead: use when a paused lead should continue
- Mark as block: use for permanent no-contact situations
- Do not confuse Not Interested (categorized outcome) with Mark as block (system-level stop)

### Replace Lead -- use when:
- Current contact is the wrong person
- OOO auto-reply gives an alternate contact
- Someone forwards you to the correct person
- A better direct contact is provided

Info needed for Replace Lead: First Name, Last Name, Email (required), Company Website, LinkedIn Profile, Company Name, Phone Number

Replace Lead rules:
- Only replace when a real new contact is provided
- Double check the email before confirming
- Once replaced, all future communication transfers to the new lead

### Resume Lead -- use when:
- Lead was marked OOO and did not automatically resume
- Campaign needs to continue after pause
- Sequence was interrupted and should continue
- Resume only when the reason for pause is gone
- Check latest message before resuming

### Mark as Block -- use for:
- Do Not Contact
- Explicit removal requests
- Legal threats
- Hostile replies
- Clear requests to stop outreach permanently
- If lead says remove me, stop emailing, unsubscribe, or makes a legal complaint -- mark as block immediately
- Do not send any additional reply after blocking

### STATUS 8: OUT OF OFFICE

Automated OOO reply. Read for return date. Follow up 1-2 days after return.

Action logic:
- Return date only → wait, then Resume Lead when appropriate
- Alternate contact provided → evaluate, then Replace Lead if valid
- No useful info → wait and resume later
- If extended leave with alternate contact → treat as Wrong Person

Or just click Resume Lead in Smartlead so the campaign scripts continue.

## SOP 3: Calendly & Scheduling Protocol

Always propose 2 specific time slots first -- feels more personal and easier to respond to.

Tagging a lead as Meeting Request automatically adds them to subsequence. 1st follow-up message will be sent after 1 day if lead doesn't respond.

Format: "I'm free Wed 11am or 3pm and Fri 1pm or 4pm EST, either works?"

Times must be at least 2 days from today's date. Always give times in EST.

**Scenario A -- They pick one of your times:**
→ Manually add to Calendly. Change status to Booked. No Calendly link needed.

**Scenario B -- They want a far future date:**
→ Manually add to Calendly. Change status to Booked.

**Scenario C -- No response after 24 hours:**
→ Go to Master Inbox. Open lead thread. Change Lead Category to Meeting Request.
→ Lead is handled through subsequence follow-up flow.

**Scenario D -- They ask for different times or the link:**
→ Send Calendly link immediately.
→ Link: https://calendly.com/jan__w/partnership_itssimannn
→ Update Lead Category to Meeting Request.

**Scenario E -- They book through Calendly:**
→ Open lead thread. Change Lead Category to Booked.
