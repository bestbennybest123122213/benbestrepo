## Tool Deep Dives

### Apollo.io ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**What it does:** B2B database with 210M+ contacts, built-in email sequences

**Why it's great for ItssIMANNN:**
- Filter by industry: Gaming, Mobile Apps, Tech Startups
- Filter by funding: Recently funded (VC-backed companies!)
- Filter by company size: 50-500 employees (sweet spot)
- Filter by tech stack: Unity, Unreal, mobile SDK users
- Built-in outreach sequences (can replace SmartLead eventually)

**Pricing:**
- Free: 600 email credits/yr
- Basic: $49/mo - 12,000 credits/yr, sequences
- Professional: $99/mo - unlimited sequences, intent data
- Organization: $149/mo - advanced filters, buying intent

**Gaming-specific filters:**
- Industry: "Computer Games", "Video Games", "Mobile Games"
- Keywords: "game studio", "mobile gaming", "esports"
- Technologies: Unity, Unreal Engine, AWS GameLift

### Clay ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**What it does:** Data enrichment + AI-powered lead research at scale

**Why it's great for ItssIMANNN:**
- **Waterfall enrichment**: Tries multiple data sources until it finds the info
- **AI research**: Can scrape company websites for specific info
- **100+ integrations**: Apollo, LinkedIn, Clearbit, etc.
- **Automation tables**: Build complex lead scoring workflows

**Pricing:**
- Starter: $149/mo - 5,000 credits
- Explorer: $349/mo - 30,000 credits
- Pro: $800/mo - 150,000 credits

**Use case for Jan:**
1. Import gaming companies from Apollo
2. Enrich with: recent funding news, tech stack, employee count
3. AI-research: Find marketing decision-makers
4. Score and prioritize leads
5. Auto-export to SmartLead

### Ocean.io ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**What it does:** AI lookalike search - find companies similar to your best customers

**Why it's great for ItssIMANNN:**
- Upload your 44 booked meetings
- AI finds similar companies (same size, industry, signals)
- Precision targeting = higher conversion

**Pricing:** ~$299-599/mo (contact for gaming-specific plans)

**Best for:**
- When you have a clear ICP and want MORE of the same
- Finding hidden gems (smaller studios that match the pattern)
