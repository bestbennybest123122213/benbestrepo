## 💡 USAGE TIPS

1. **Always personalize** - Reference specific news, products, or conversations
2. **Lead with results** - 100K users, 3% CTR, 30% download rates
3. **Match the tone** - Gaming = casual, Tech = professional, Entertainment = creative
4. **One clear CTA** - Calendar link or "quick call?"
5. **Keep it short** - 4-6 sentences max
